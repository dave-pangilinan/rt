    General Tips

    Moving around this islands and its municipalities will take you to an enigma of wonders and here are some tips to consider.

    • Dress Free - The year round temperature ranges from 24 -29 degrees celsius. Casual dress is appropriate all year round in the tropical Philippine weather. It is especially ideal to wear clothing in light cotion fabric.
    • Be Light As A Feather - Limit the things you carry with you throughout the trip. Just bring basic travelling necessities and your own special personal needs. There are lot of marble products and other novelty items that you can buy.
    • Be A Boy Scout or Girl Scout - The islands and its municipalities has a very low crime rate if compared to other cities and municipalities. However, It is a good idea to use a belt-bag to put your money, passport and other valuables when on the road rather that a big bag for safety purposes.
    • Have An Eagle Eye - Be aware of where you leave your bags and other belongings in public places. It might be safer to leave them in your hotel safe.
    • Don't Be Afraid - Romblomanons are very courteous, friendly and welcoming people. Don't be atraid to smile, greet them start conversation with them.

    Language

    There are three major dialects in the islands: Asi, Onhan and Ini but Filipino and English is widely spoken.

    Talk the Talk: Romblomanon Lingo (Ini)

    • Yes - Hu-o
    • No - Bukon
    • Stop - Para
    • Good Morning - Maayo nga Aga
    • Good Afternoon - Maayo nga Hapon
    • Good Evening - Maayo nga Gab-i
    • How are you? - Kamusta?
    • How much? - Tig pila?
    • What time is it? - Anong oras na?
    • Thank you - Salamat gid
    • Where is the (location)? - Diin ngani ang (location)?
    • Delicious - Kanamit
    • I’m hungry! - Ginagutom ako!
    • Breakfast - Pamahaw
    • Lunch - Igma
    • Dinner - Ihapon
    • Let’s eat! - Kari makaon!
    • Right - Tu-o
    • Left - Waya
    • What’s your name? - Ano ang imo ngayan?
