About

This app was made through the vision and guidance of my father, Ramon Magallon Maestro.

For his beloved hometown.

Romblon Islands Guide is an app for tourists and locals to promote the province’s cultural heritage,
tourist attractions, accommodations and dining places.

This app is not-for-profit and is made specially for Romblon Islands Tourism.

Help us add more content. If you have visited Romblon Islands and want your photos and descriptions of attractions, accomodations and dining places to be featured on this app, you may contact us via Facebook or Email.

Help us improve accuracy by suggesting edits on outdated or incorrect content.
