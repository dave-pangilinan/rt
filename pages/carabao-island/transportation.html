Sea Transportation

The port of Batangas is the main gateway that connects the islands from Luzon. It caters ships en route to Romblon, Odiongan and San Agustin. If a sea vessel is more your style, you can access two passenger ships traveling from Batangas port to seaports of Romblon. 2GO ferries or Montenegro shipping lines from Batangas Port.  Batangas Port is a 2 hour comfortable air-condition bus trip from Manila/Makati LRT Bus terminals.

Air Transportation

Tablas Domestic Airport in Tugdan, Alcantara is the only entryway to the province via air. Cebu Pacific connects Manila to Tablas and chartered flights from Kalibo connect Panay Island to Tablas and Sibuyan. Cebu Pacific has flights every week. For an updated information about flights to Tablas, please visit www.cebupacificair.com.

Van & Scooter Rental

Get to your destination comfortably by renting scooter or vans. Most of our accommodations establishments offer van rentals. You can also contact the local tourism office for van and scooter shops list.

Public Transportation

Each municipality within and island is connected by public utility jeepneys, motor-taxis or tricycles. Terminals are located beside the Public Market of each town. It is typical to have a scheduled trip per route on a daily basis. For an updated information about public transportation schedules, please contact the local tourism.

Driving In The Philippines

Tourists who hold a driver license issued by the road authority of their home country are allowed to use that license to drive in the Philippines for a period of until 90 days after their arrival in the Philippines If their driver license is not written in English, it should always be accompanied by an official English translation issued by their country's embassy in the Philippines.
